Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is a Portuguese professional footballer. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

He was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Ronaldo is known for his incredible goal-scoring record, athleticism, and dedication to the sport.

He has played for several top clubs, including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. He later returned to Manchester United and now plays for Al-Nassr in Saudi Arabia.

For the Portuguese national team, he is the all-time leading goal scorer and has captained his country to victory in the 2016 European Championship.
